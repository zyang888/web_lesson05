Foodie Reviews

All the off the menu food we've had has been good, among the best in town, but it is authentic - so if you're thinking fried rice should be thick with soy sauce flavor and running with pea and carrot medley; this is not your kind of chinese. If you know that char-zsu should be ruby red and juicy, tender and flavorful rather than a hard grey-white pork slab coated in glow-in-the-dark red served with mustard - you will enjoy your orders here.
